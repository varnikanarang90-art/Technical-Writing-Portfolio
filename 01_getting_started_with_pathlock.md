# Getting Started with Pathlock  
*A Sample User Guide*

## Overview  
This guide introduces new business users to Pathlock and helps them complete their first access-related task. Pathlock is a platform that helps organizations manage user access, ensure compliance, and reduce risks across applications.

## Who This Guide Is For  
- Business users receiving access review tasks  
- Managers who approve access requests  
- New users logging into Pathlock for the first time

## Prerequisites  
- You have received your login credentials  
- You can access your organization’s SSO login page  
- You have an active corporate email

---

## Step 1: Log In  
1. Navigate to your organization’s Pathlock login URL.  
2. Enter your SSO credentials.  
3. Complete multi-factor authentication if prompted.  
4. You will arrive on the Pathlock home dashboard.

---

## Step 2: Explore the Dashboard  
The dashboard may include:  
- **Pending Reviews** – tasks requiring your approval  
- **Access Requests** – users requesting access  
- **Risk Alerts** – potential compliance or access issues  
- **Recent Activity** – your completed tasks

---

## Step 3: Complete Your First Access Review  
1. Open **Pending Reviews**.  
2. Select a user who needs their access approved or revoked.  
3. Review their access details.  
4. Choose **Approve** or **Reject**.  
5. Add a justification if required.  
6. Click **Submit**.

---

## Step 4: Getting Help  
If you need assistance, contact your internal GRC or IT security team.
